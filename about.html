About Us

Our Goals, Ambitions and What we Stand For

Our Goals

We strive to provide professional advice and consultations regarding any travel issues, as we can provide you with the latest travel news and Visa application help to ensure you have prepared the right and necessary documents to enjoy travelling abroad for business, study and travel purposes. More information can be provided on our website.

Our main aim is to provide as many overseas placements as possible for all ethnicities and ages, whilst providing any support you feel you may need at every step of the way, we also prioritise finding the right placement that fits you perfectly, find your placement today, we know you do not want to miss out on this amazing opportunity, reach out today to know what your options are.

Who We Are

Vasave Overseas Placement provides excellent Visa consultations and helps those who would like to study overseas, travel and conduct business. We also provide courses in overseas placement opportunities in different countries such as the UK, UAE, USA and Canada. Where you can learn, earn and travel!

We are a collective of passionate professionals who are willing to share their expert knowledge and advice that has been gained through providing their services for many years working in different industries.
