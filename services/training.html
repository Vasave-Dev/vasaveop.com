Networking Courses

CCMA Training

What is it?

CCNA stands for Cisco Certified Network Associate, It recognizes basic skills in installing, supporting, and troubleshooting wired or wireless networks. One can choose from several tracks, including Cloud, Collaboration, Cyber Ops, Data Center, Industrial, Routing and Switching, Security, Service Provider, and Wireless. It is an entry-level certificate, for individuals who want a foundational understanding of:

  • Topics in IT Networking
  • Information in relation to Cisco technology and products
Why should you aim for a certificate in CCNA
  • It is recognized and accepted globally
  • Once an individual possesses a CCNA certificate, it is easier to get a job
  • Once an individual possesses a CCNA certificate, it is easier to get a job; The knowledge ascertained through the CCNA certification can be proficiently used for studying other networking modules, such as CCNA Security and CCNA wireless.
