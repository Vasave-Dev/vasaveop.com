One can choose from several tracks, including Cloud, Collaboration, Cyber Ops, Data Center, Industrial, Routing and Switching, Security, Service Provider, and Wireless. It is an entry-level certificate, for individuals who want a foundational understanding of:

  • Once an individual possesses a CCNA certificate, it is easier to get a job.

Ethical Hacking Courses

Who is this for?

It is for individuals who are pursuing a career in ethical hacking, it helps the individual to understand and gain hands-on experience on how to identify, counteract and prevent security threats within an organization’s IT system.

The Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) is a professional designation for a person who has obtained a qualification that allows them to perform specific services for various companies. As their name would suggest, ethical hackers are certified personnel qualified to penetrate a company’s computer systems (by their permission) in an attempt at determining whether there are any vulnerabilities. Additionally, an ethical hacker will also undertake all preventive and corrective measures needed to protect the system against any real attacks that may happen in the future.

Reasons to get Certified Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking allows an individual to gain knowledge that is linked to a have an understanding of the organization’s current network infrastructure and then report any vulnerabilities within the network. Having a CEH shows that the individual has an understanding within:

  • Linux
  • Reconnaissance
  • Network Hacking
  • System Hacking
  • Web Hacking
  • Denial Of Service
  • Hacking web servers/Applications
  • IoT Hacking
  • Server/Client-side attack
  • Cryptography
  • Social Engineering

Do you need Ethical Hacking training?

There is specific training that is needed to be an Ethical Hacker. An individual needs extensive knowledge of various platforms, Networking & programming (Basic). The individual also needs to understand the vulnerabilities of said systems and how they are exploited by cybercriminals. It is also advised that individuals that achieve certification within ethical hacking, must apply and gain approval from the Communications-Electronics Security Group.

The examination that is needed to gain a Certificate in Ethical Hacking

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) by EC-Council will teach you the latest commercial-grade hacking tools, techniques, and methodologies used by hackers and information security professionals to lawfully hack an organization. There are basically five options to get training from EC-Council which are iLearn (Self-Study), iWeek (Live Online), Master Class, Training Partner (In Person), Education Partner (In-Person or Online).

The examination is provided by the EC-Council which is globally recognized and officially collaborated with UK Government standards. There are three types of certifications available for ethical hacking which are Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), CEH Practical, CEH Master.

Prerequisite to becoming Ethical Hacker

To be successful in ethical hacking, you'll need to develop a wide range of skills. There are technical skills such as understanding TCP/IP and writing code, and softer skills, such as understanding how people behave when using computers. You should have CompTIA A+ and Network+, or equivalent experience under your belt. Having these two certifications before CEH will gain the basic skills and boost you to pass the CEH exam however it is not compulsory.

Cyber security courses

Who is this for?

The cyber security training course is for individuals who want to implement systems, securely and safely. While avoiding introduction to threats within the organization’s network infrastructure. Individuals will cover topics that are based on the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP). This will help them to understand how to harden the code and make sure that you are not implementing unintentional risk within the current system.

Reasons to get certified in Cyber Security training?

Cyber security training and certification provide the individual understanding regarding current threats that the company currently faces. In today’s environment, more companies are adopting cloud-based technology within their system. The security certification grows more steadily within the career field of DevOps and app developers.

Do you need Cyber Security training?

Yes, companies and individuals are attending Cyber security training to have a better understanding of today’s digital landscape. It is not a new concept for medium to large-size organizations. This is due to the fact that since the introduction of the internet, there are risks to personal and business security.

The examination that is needed to gain a Cyber Security Certificate Examination that is provided by the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) or the also known as (ISC)², is highly recognized and should be sought after if individuals are planning to do personal study.

Certification Route

Path of getting a CISSP Certification
  • Stage 1: Ensure this is the right certification for you and your needs.
  • Stage 2: Register/enroll and prepare for the exam.
CISSP Domains
  • Security and Risk Management
  • Asset Security
  • Security Architecture and Engineering
  • Communication and Network Security
  • Identity and Access Management (IAM)
  • Security Assessment and Testing
  • Security Operations
  • Software Development Security
Stage 3: Get Certified

To qualify for this cybersecurity certification, you must pass the exam and have at least 5 years of cumulative, paid work experience in two or more of the eight domains.

There are 2 possible “work experience” variations, one is full-time and the second is part-time. Full time would require a 35h/week for 4 weeks to get a month of work experience. Part-time would require no less than 20h/week and no more than 34h/week for 4 weeks to acquire a month of work experience.
