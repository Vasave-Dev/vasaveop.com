Overseas Placements

Giving all people the opportunity to work, study and establish residency in multiple destinations from around the world

Placement opportunities

We are thrilled to announce we have job placement opportunities available all around the globe. Why not start buffing out your CV or resume with job opportunities straight away. You will gain the skills, qualification, experience and open doors to bigger and better things, after laying the foundation with our study and work programs. The possibilities are endless. Who knows, you may even pick up a new language!

Consultations

We know it can be confusing and difficult to sort out all the procedures and documentation needed to start to think about studying, working or establishing residency overseas. However, we have an excellent team of consultants ready and eager to help. We can help you over the phone or through face to face virtual consultations with you, so you can feel comfortable talking to a person and not at a prerecorded message option.

We can provide you with any career advice and guidance throughout your course with us and answer any questions you have along the way.

We are also dedicated to helping you and advising you on the proper way to fill out your visa application form, for any purpose of travel you may have. We will support you throughout your entire journey, from making sure you have made the correct preparation for applying to helping you with any travel issues you may face while you are abroad.
