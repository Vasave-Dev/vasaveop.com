Design

From Graphic Design to UI and UX Design, get involved in the Digital Design industry

Graphic Design

This course is designed to create visual content by using page layouts techniques, this is used to communicate messages from the design to the viewers, this is to give the viewers maximum experience of what has been created.

UX/UI Design

This course is designed to bring a design-centric approach to user interface and user experience. This course is mainly centered around visual communications but still is taught about marketing, programming on mobile devices.

Web Design

This course draws on many different disciplines in order to create and maintain websites that run smoothly and to a high standard. With the course specifically covering the concepts of Colour Theory, Law of Design, among a few others and then demonstrating the implementation of them through Shape Tools, Lasso Tools, Clipping Masking and much more. The aim of the course is to develop confident and capable designers that have a number of skills and techniques at their disposal.
